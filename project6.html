Sea Sepherd Redesign

Sea Sepherd homepage

This project is a redesign of the Sea Shepherd homepage. The main goal was to bring clarity to this website so users (and potential contributors !) can really focus on the content.

Dl

The donation feature is now displayed on the homepage, because it's a main feature of the website. You can choose between the monthly donation and the punctual one. The donation explanation is highlighted, to make sure the donator really understands the use of their contribution.

Dl

The user can choose to join the crew or to volunteer in any way. Those are two possibilities. On the original website, they are one, and appear at the same level as the shop and the donation card. Finally, I added a newsletter bar to allow users to follow the organization's news and activity.

Budget App screens

This is a personal project. I imagined an app for budgets and accounts. I made a few screens from the dashboard to the wallet. The intention was to add some fun and colors to a financial, complicated app. This way, the user can deal with their money more easily.

Dl

The user can send money to their friends and relatives very quickly. Colorful graphs allow them to have a global view of their account and balance (spent/earned). Tips for savings and all kind of banking advices are displayed at the bottom of the Wallet screen. This app helps you in your money life, especially if you don't know much about finance.
